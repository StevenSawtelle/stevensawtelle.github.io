Like sudoku puzzles, but dont't find them challenging enough? Enter my take on the classic: the Sudokube!
Sudokubes are made up of 6 smaller Sudoku puzzles each placed onto a face of a cube, and the edged/corners of matching faces have shared numbers. For example, if you have 1 in the bottom right corner of the puzzle on top face of the cube, that will mean there must also be a 1 in the top right corner of the front face (and even a 1 in the top left corner of the right face!)
I hope you enjoy playing around with my puzzle idea, and feel free to check it out on Github if you are interested in learning more about how it's made!
Currently supported options: zoom , pan, rotate, highlight, filling in empty squares (with keyboard)
Work in Progress features: filling in empty squares (without keyboard), answer verification, regenerating new puzzle freely
